Thought I finally seized the edge

In a cage, with key in the pocket wearing an attire with no pockets

They own the keys, they own the cage, they sit on their thrones with their rusty lockets

Ohh! Privilege of their sanctimonious greed

I can sense their witty smile; As I shed the tears of ordeal

Clawing through their bluest flames for the key

With my hopes dying in their cold decree

Gazed at me with their sympathetic eyes

And gave the key as a frozen prize

My heart whispered "That's a trick"

Crept towards the lock with the freedom in my hands

only to realize the lock was my biometric

The Whispers of the Galaxies

You hear my silent roar,

As the cosmic sinkhole within me gnaws at my core.

I'm the galaxy;

You ruminate to feed your space fantasy.

You frame me as elliptical;

While the veins in my arms scream in a spiral.

Your telescope tries;

Fueled by passion, you bound to commit,

You pace in your pen and I pace in my orbit.

Your ink screams in blue to its final extent;

Too far from me to ever know my present.

In aftermath; it turns out to be one of your illusions,

A forever relic to my past allusions.

Were you my Jupiter?

With the bill of my deferred dreams due

Knocked on a door carved to refuse

Though with bricks laid to drown my screams

Passing right by you the meteor hit me hard

Scar turns into scars

Did you protect me from the hail storm of meteors?

Did you make me feel ME?

Critical to Escape

Was tied by your gravitational pull to be the Peter Pan of the cohort

Was I at fault for leaving your orbit and the rest stranded?

Yearned to see the apricity while being covered in your dust storms

cause of what you inculcated in me and the rest
